The challenge

Most PE firms do not have an AI problem. They have a rollout problem.

Portfolio leaders know AI needs to be in the business. The harder part is deciding where it fits, how to deploy it across multiple locations, and how to maintain consistency without slowing down local teams.

Fragmented visibility

Different locations market differently, follow up differently, and perform differently. Leadership lacks one clean way to monitor what is happening across the portfolio.

Generic AI does not get adopted

Most AI tools are too broad, too abstract, or too disconnected from daily workflows. Local teams do not need another system. They need something built around how the business actually runs.

Inconsistency creates revenue leakage

Missed calls, uneven review generation, weak local search presence, and poor follow-up all add up across dozens of locations. Without a repeatable system, value creation becomes slower and less predictable.
